# Hi, I'm Moses Thlama James

**Edge AI Hardware Engineer | Embedded Systems | Electronics**

I'm an electronics engineer working at the intersection of hardware and AI. My day-to-day involves researching and configuring edge AI hardware, designing systems with CAD tools, and solving the problem of getting AI models to run reliably on physical devices in real-world environments.

I hold an MSc in Electronic Communication and Computer Engineering from the University of Nottingham and a BEng in Computer Engineering.

## What I work on

- **Edge AI hardware** — researching, configuring, and optimising devices for on-device AI inference
- **PCB design and rework** — circuit analysis, fault diagnosis, and hardware prototyping
- **Embedded systems** — firmware, microcontrollers, signal processing, ADC/DAC
- **Automation** — building scripts and tools to streamline hardware testing and configuration workflows
